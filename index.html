What Simplifii is

Not a cheat.
A translator.

Simplifii does not write your essays. It does not complete your assignments. It does not generate submissions.

It does what every university should be doing but doesn't: explains what is being asked in plain language, maps out exactly how to approach it, and gives you the scaffolding to do your own best work.

Using Simplifii is no different from visiting a learning support centre or asking a tutor to explain an assessment brief. Except Simplifii is available at 2am, never makes you feel stupid, and was designed specifically for neurodivergent students.

Why four versions?

Neuroinclusive design is not one-size-fits-all. Different students need different things. By building four parallel versions and gathering real outcome data, we're not guessing what works — we're measuring it. The data from all four builds feeds into Simplifii 2.0 — the definitive neuroinclusive assessment platform. Every student who uses any version is contributing to research that will change how universities support diverse learners.
